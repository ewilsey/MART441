Tubersome Grin
  As a self-aware potato, your only mission is to survive.

Simple? Many obstacles stand in your path. Many foes stand

to gain noteriety from your defeat. Will you become the

baked potato you were once destined to become? Or will you

rise above and transform into something much greater?

The choice is yours, but yours alone. Stay or Go?
A Choose-Your-Own Story by Eric Wilsey
